About Us

Innovating the Future with Robotics

SARS Robotics is a visionary research and development team committed to revolutionizing the field of robotics through creative innovation, interdisciplinary collaboration, and hands-on education. Established in 2011, SARS has been at the forefront of technological advancement for over a decade.

Our team brings together passionate minds from engineering, computer science, and design to develop intelligent robotic systems that tackle real-world problems. Through national competitions, technical workshops, and community outreach programs, we aim to inspire and empower the next generation of tech leaders while making robotics accessible and impactful for all.

Our Achievements

Successfully Competed at IIT Bhubaneswar

Our participation at IIT Bhubaneswar marked a major milestone in our journey. Presenting our advanced robotic systems at a nationally recognized platform allowed us to engage with industry experts, receive valuable feedback, and showcase our technical prowess. Competing alongside some of the brightest engineering minds in the country not only challenged us but also validated our work in innovation and automation.

Successfully Competed at IIT Kharagpur

At the Techno-Management Fest of IIT Kharagpur—one of India’s most prestigious technical events—our team stood out with creative, real-world robotic solutions. The experience was a blend of innovation, intense competition, and collaboration, helping us further solidify our presence in the Indian robotics landscape. It opened doors for networking, recognition, and future partnerships.

4 Successful Workshops in 2024

In 2024, we organized four hands-on robotics workshops that collectively attracted over 400 participants from different colleges and schools. These workshops emphasized practical learning and inspired a new wave of tech enthusiasts. From assembling bots to programming autonomous behavior, each session empowered participants with foundational skills and confidence to explore the world of robotics.

Initiated Wireless Bot Architecture

We developed and deployed a cutting-edge wireless bot architecture—an innovation that eliminated the need for traditional wired control systems. This project redefined how our robots interact, paving the way for seamless, responsive, and remote-controlled operations. It is a foundational step toward creating highly flexible and mobile robotics systems suitable for real-time applications in smart cities, defense, and agriculture.
