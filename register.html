Simulations Based on IoT Devices Workshop

Organized by: SARS Club

Dates: March 17th, 18th, and 19th

Venue: Smart Class 1 & Smart Class 2

Expected Participants: 200 students

Event Description

The SARS Club is hosting a three-day workshop to introduce first-year students to the fundamentals of IoT and its simulation techniques. The workshop will include theoretical sessions and hands-on practice using IoT simulation tools.

Instructions for Participants

  • Venue Setup & Equipment Preparation: Ensure projectors, computers, and internet connectivity are set up. IoT simulation software should be pre-installed.
  • Registration & Attendance: Participants must register before the sessions. Attendance will be recorded for documentation.
  • Workshop Structure:
    • Day 1: Introduction to IoT and its applications.
    • Day 2: Hands-on practice with IoT simulators.
    • Day 3: Advanced simulations and real-world applications.
  • Discipline & Guidelines: Follow university rules, avoid disturbances, and report any technical issues to the organizing team.
  • Feedback & Certification: Complete a feedback form to receive a certificate of participation.
